Drip... drip... drip...

You awake blearily, a groan escaping your lips involuntarily as your eyes try to focus in the dim light.
The rough rock floor beneath your cheek is uneven, pocketed with water erosion and a creeping dull green moss.
The source of the flickering light seems to be a slight depression in the floor, ringed by moss hungrily soaking up the feeble light spilling over the shallow stone lip.

Get to your feet.
