Gingerly rising to your feet, you check yourself for any outstanding injuries.
Content that, while certainly bruised and shaken, you are none the worse for wear, you peer into the hollow from which the light is emanating.
A pitiful flame smolders amongst a handful of damp kindling, hardly enough to provide any warmth, but sufficient to illuminate your view for several yards around you.

Look around.
